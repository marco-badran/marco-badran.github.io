Since 2019 I am a Ph.D. student at University of Bath under the supervision of Manuel del Pino. From September 2023, I will be a postdoctoral fellow at ETH Zürich.

I am mainly interested in Geometric PDEs and Differential Geometry. In particular, I work with minimal surfaces and their relation with the PDE framework. I am also interested in Ginzburg-Landau theory, nonlocal minimal surfaces and harmonic maps (both classical and fractional).
